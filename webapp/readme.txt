AUTHORS: Claire Williams and Luisa Escosteguy

DATA: The Kaggle dataset Video Games Sales with Ratings contains a list 
    of video games with sales greater than 100,000 copies, including 
    information such as game name, platform, release year, genre, publisher, 
    and various statistics about sales. 

The dataset is available to the public at https://www.kaggle.com/rush4ratio/video-game-sales-with-ratings 
    (Video_Games_Sales_as_at_22_Dec_2016.csv)
